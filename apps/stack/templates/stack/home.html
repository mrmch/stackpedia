what?

stackpedia aspires to be an easy way for developers to list the software projects they are using in their "stack".

how?

You build your stack by telling us what projects you use. We give you a pretty d3.js visual you can share.

that's it?

We try to pull data from github to tell you who's contributing on the projects you're using, and we look at the licenses to see if you're compliant =]
